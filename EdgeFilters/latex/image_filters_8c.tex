Creates a Do\+G filter with {\itshape dim} columns and {\itshape dim} rows. 

This filter is the result of the difference of two equal Gaussian filters with equal size and different sigmas. The external gaussian has sigma set by {\itshape sigma}. The internal gaussian has a smaller sigma equal to {\itshape sigma} / 1.\+66 .
