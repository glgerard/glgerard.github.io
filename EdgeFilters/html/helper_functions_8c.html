int quadrant ( int  a)

Return one of four quadrants where the angle a falls. The angle is in degree.

The angle a is categorized in one of the following 4 quadrants:

  • 0: [ -22.5, +22.5] or [+157.5, +180.0] or [-157.5, -180.0]
  • 1: [ +22.5, +67.5] or [-157.5, -112.5]
  • 2: [ +67.5, +112.5] or [-112.5, -67.5]
  • 3: [+112.5, +157.5] or [ -67.5, -22.5]
